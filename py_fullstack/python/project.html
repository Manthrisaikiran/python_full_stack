Promoting Gender Equality

Empowering everyone to achieve equality and justice.

Guidance

Gender equality means that the different behaviors, aspirations, and needs of women and men are considered, valued, and favored equally. It doesn't mean that women and men have to become the same, but their rights, responsibilities, and opportunities will not depend on whether they are born male or female.

Rules

Here are some key rules for promoting gender equality:

  • Ensure equal access to education for all genders.
  • Promote equal pay for equal work.
  • Encourage women to participate in decision-making processes.
  • Prevent and respond to gender-based violence.
  • Support policies and laws that promote gender equality.
